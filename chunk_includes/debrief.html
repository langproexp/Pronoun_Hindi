Debrief

    Thank you for taking part in the current study.

  1. Research Project Title: Referential communication

  2. Aims of project

    The primary aim of this experiment was to investigate the factors that influence the speaker’s choice of referential forms. In particular, we are interested in how speakers refer to the objects in the visual array.

  1. What happens to the data I provided?

    We will transcribe the participant’s speech and analyse the choice of referring expressions. The research data will be kept anonymously and confidentially on the university central server.

  1. Will the research be published?

    The research may be published in journal articles and conference proceedings. You will not be identifiable in any report or publication.

  1. Contact Details

    If you wish your data to be removed, you must email Kumiko Fukumura (kumiko.fukumura@stir.ac.uk) within 7 days of participating in the study.

    If you have any questions about the research or would like to receive a summary of our findings, please feel free to contact myself at email kumiko.fukumura@stir.ac.uk.
